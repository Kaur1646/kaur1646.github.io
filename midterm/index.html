Facts of whalefish

  • They live in the inky depths below 1,000 meters (3,300 feet) called the midnight zone.
  • Their eyes are poorly developed and lack lenses.
  • Whalefish coloration is typically red to orange, sometimes with a black body
  • Some species possess light-producing organs called photophores
